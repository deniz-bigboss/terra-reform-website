terra-reform.org Gets a Major Upgrade

Our website now features an interactive project timeline, a recognition overview, working contact and application forms, light and dark themes, and a newsletter — making it easier than ever to follow our work and get involved.

Solar-Powered School Microgrids Initiative Kicks Off

Our newest project, developed through the New York Academy of Sciences challenge program, investigates how solar generation, battery storage, and smart energy management can turn schools into clean energy hubs and community resilience anchors. Work runs through May 2026.

Superconductor Research Proposal Submitted to CERN BL4S

Team Spartacus completed "Superconductivity Preservation in NbN Thin Films: The Role of Lead Coating Under Proton Irradiation" — our most ambitious research effort yet — and submitted it to the CERN Beamline for Schools 2025 competition.

Two Projects Head to TEKNOFEST

Braille Circuits, our tactile electronics learning kit for visually impaired students, and Safe Explorer, a radio-based emergency communication concept for remote environments, both completed their development cycles for TEKNOFEST competitions.
