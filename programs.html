Competitions & Programs

Where We've Competed

National and international competitions and programs across science, technology, engineering, and policy.

01

TEKNOFEST

Turkey's largest technology and aerospace festival. Terra Reform has developed multiple engineering and technology projects — Safe Explorer, Braille Circuits, and Virtual Physics Laboratory — for TEKNOFEST competitions.

02

TÜBİTAK

High school research project competitions by The Scientific and Technological Research Council of Turkey. Virtual Physics Laboratory was a Regional Finalist in the TÜBİTAK 2204-A competition, and AIR was developed for the same TÜBİTAK 2204-A track.

03

CERN Beamline for Schools

An international competition hosted by CERN inviting students to propose experiments using a real CERN particle beam, encouraging advanced physics and experimental design thinking.

04

New York Academy of Sciences

A Global STEM Alliance challenge series connecting student innovators internationally. Terra Reform developed the Solar-Powered School Microgrid project through the NYAS program.

05

Büyük Ankara AI & Climate Policy Workshop

A policy-oriented program exploring the intersection of artificial intelligence, climate change, and governance — connecting student thinkers with real policy challenges.
