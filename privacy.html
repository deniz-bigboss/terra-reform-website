Privacy Notice

Short version: we only collect what you choose to send us, we never sell it, and you can ask us to delete it at any time.

What We Collect

When you use our contact form, application form, or newsletter signup, we receive the information you enter — typically your name, email address, and message. We do not collect anything you don't explicitly type in.

How It's Processed

Form submissions are delivered to our inbox by Formspree, which processes the data on our behalf (see Formspree's privacy policy). We use your information only to reply to you, process your application, or send occasional newsletter updates — whichever you asked for.

Analytics

We use GoatCounter, a privacy-friendly analytics tool that counts page visits without cookies and without tracking you across the web. No personal profiles are built.

What We Don't Do

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing. We do not use advertising trackers or cross-site cookies.

Your Rights

You can ask us at any time to see, correct, or delete the personal data we hold about you, or to unsubscribe from updates. Just email info@terra-reform.org and we'll take care of it.
