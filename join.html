Join Terra Reform

Terra Reform welcomes motivated students interested in science, engineering, software development, research, and sustainability. We are an international initiative and welcome students from all backgrounds, disciplines, and countries.

Who We're Looking For

Find Your Place

Whether you're passionate about clean energy, robotics, accessibility, or scientific research — there's a place for you here.

Researchers

Students who enjoy investigating open questions, designing experiments, and writing up findings — physics, materials science, environmental science, and beyond.

Engineers & Builders

Students who like designing and prototyping hardware — from robotics platforms to communication devices and educational kits.

Developers

Students who can build software, simulations, and tools — including websites, applications, and interactive learning environments.

Sustainability Advocates

Students focused on environmental and societal challenges, from clean energy systems to community resilience.

Educators & Communicators

Students interested in making science and technology more accessible — through teaching, content, and outreach.

Designers

Students who care about accessibility and inclusive design — making sure our projects work for everyone.
